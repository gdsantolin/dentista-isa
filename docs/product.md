# Product Vision

## Purpose

Create a professional website to showcase the work of dentist Isabella.

The website should communicate trust, professionalism, and approachability.

Initially, it represents only the professional.

In the future, it may evolve into the official website of her own dental clinic.

---

## Target Audience

People looking for dental treatments such as:

- Preventive care
- Teeth cleaning
- Whitening
- Restorations
- Veneers
- Implants
- General dental consultation

The main audience is located in the city where she currently practices.

---

## Primary Goals

- Present the professional
- Build credibility
- Explain available treatments
- Make it easy to contact via WhatsApp
- Improve online presence
- Rank well on Google

---

## MVP Features

- Single-page website
- Professional photo
- About section
- Services section
- WhatsApp button
- Practice locations
- Instagram link
- Contact section
- Footer

---

## Future Features

- Blog
- Appointment scheduling
- Admin dashboard
- Testimonials
- Before and after gallery
- Multi-clinic support
